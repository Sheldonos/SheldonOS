## Cold Call Scripts

### Script 1: For [Top Category 1] & [Top Category 2]

We build AI systems that automate reminders via text, email, and voice, and we're seeing practices like yours recover $5,000–$10,000 a month in lost revenue. Do you have 15 minutes on Thursday to see how it works?"

## 15-Minute Demo Script

1.  **(2 min) Intro & Agenda:** "Hi [Name], thanks for the time. The goal of this call is to show you how we can solve [Pain Point] and generate an extra [$X] in revenue for [Business Name]. I'll show you a quick demo, then we can see if it's a fit. Sound good?"

2.  **(3 min) Problem Discovery:** "You mentioned you're struggling with [Pain Point]. How many hours a week does your team spend on that? What's the cost of that in your opinion?"

3.  **(5 min) Solution Demo:** "Great. Here's how we solve that. (Share screen and show the [Tool Name] dashboard). Instead of your team doing [manual task], our AI does it automatically. Here's a live example... See how it [specific action]? This runs 24/7."

4.  **(3 min) ROI & Pricing:** "Based on what you told me, this system would save you [X hours/week] and recover [$Y in lost revenue]. Our pricing for this is a one-time setup of [$Setup Fee] and [$Monthly Fee]/month. Most of our clients see a full return on their investment in under 90 days."

5.  **(2 min) Next Steps:** "Does this sound like something that could help your business? If so, the next step is a 30-minute strategy session where we map out your exact implementation. I have time on [Day] or [Day] — what works for you?"
