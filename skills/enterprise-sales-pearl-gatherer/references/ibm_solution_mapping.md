# IBM Solution to Business Outcome Mapping

This reference guide maps the core IBM technology domains to universal business outcomes. Use this to translate technical capabilities into the business language of the specific Line of Business (LoB) you are targeting.

## 1. AI & Data (watsonx, Db2, Cognos)
**Technical Capabilities:** Generative AI foundation models, machine learning, data fabric, predictive analytics, data governance.
**Universal Business Outcomes:**
- **Revenue Growth:** Accelerate time-to-market for new products by mining customer data for unmet needs.
- **Operational Efficiency:** Reduce manual data entry and analysis time by 40% through automated insights.
- **Customer Experience:** Personalize customer interactions at scale, increasing retention and lifetime value.
- **Risk Mitigation:** Ensure regulatory compliance through automated data governance and lineage tracking.

## 2. Automation (RPA, Business Automation Workflow, Instana, Turbonomic)
**Technical Capabilities:** Robotic Process Automation (RPA), intelligent workflows, application performance monitoring (APM), IT resource optimization.
**Universal Business Outcomes:**
- **Cost Reduction:** Eliminate manual, repetitive tasks, reducing operational overhead by 30%.
- **Speed & Agility:** Accelerate process execution (e.g., claims processing, onboarding) from days to minutes.
- **Resilience:** Proactively identify and resolve IT bottlenecks before they impact the customer experience (Instana).
- **Resource Optimization:** Automatically allocate cloud resources to ensure performance while minimizing waste (Turbonomic).

## 3. Security (QRadar, Guardium, MaaS360, Identity)
**Technical Capabilities:** SIEM, threat detection and response, data security, endpoint management, identity and access management.
**Universal Business Outcomes:**
- **Risk Mitigation:** Prevent costly data breaches and intellectual property theft.
- **Compliance:** Automate reporting and ensure adherence to industry regulations (GDPR, HIPAA, PCI).
- **Brand Protection:** Maintain customer trust by securing sensitive data across the hybrid cloud.
- **Operational Efficiency:** Reduce the time analysts spend investigating false positives through AI-driven threat detection.

## 4. Hybrid Cloud & Infrastructure (IBM Cloud, Power Systems, Storage)
**Technical Capabilities:** Open hybrid cloud architecture, enterprise-grade servers, scalable storage, Red Hat OpenShift.
**Universal Business Outcomes:**
- **Agility:** Rapidly deploy new applications and scale resources on demand to meet market changes.
- **Cost Optimization:** Avoid vendor lock-in and optimize workload placement across public and private clouds.
- **Resilience:** Ensure high availability and disaster recovery for mission-critical applications.
- **Modernization:** Modernize legacy applications without disrupting ongoing business operations.
