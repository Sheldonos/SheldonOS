# Universal Lines of Business (LoB) Taxonomy

This reference guide provides a framework for identifying and mapping lines of business across various major industries. Use this to deconstruct the client's organization and target your IBM solutions to the specific business units that will benefit most.

## 1. Financial Services (Banking, Insurance, Capital Markets)
- **Retail Banking:** Consumer accounts, mortgages, personal loans, credit cards.
- **Commercial Banking:** Corporate lending, treasury services, trade finance.
- **Payments:** ACH, wire transfers, real-time payments, merchant services.
- **Wealth Management:** Investment advisory, private banking, trust services.
- **Insurance (P&C, Life, Health):** Underwriting, claims processing, policy administration, actuarial.

## 2. Healthcare & Life Sciences
- **Patient Care (Clinical):** Hospitals, clinics, telemedicine, electronic health records (EHR).
- **Revenue Cycle Management (Billing):** Claims processing, coding, patient billing, collections.
- **Supply Chain & Operations:** Medical equipment procurement, inventory management, facility operations.
- **Research & Development (Pharma/Biotech):** Clinical trials, drug discovery, regulatory affairs.

## 3. Retail & Consumer Goods
- **E-commerce & Digital:** Online storefronts, mobile apps, digital marketing, customer loyalty programs.
- **Store Operations:** Point of sale (POS), inventory management, workforce scheduling, loss prevention.
- **Supply Chain & Logistics:** Warehousing, distribution, transportation, demand forecasting.
- **Merchandising & Product Development:** Assortment planning, pricing strategy, product design.

## 4. Manufacturing & Automotive
- **Production & Operations:** Factory floor automation, quality control, predictive maintenance, IoT.
- **Supply Chain & Procurement:** Sourcing raw materials, supplier management, logistics.
- **Research & Development (Engineering):** Product design, prototyping, testing, lifecycle management.
- **Sales & Aftermarket Services:** Dealer networks, warranty management, spare parts, customer support.

## 5. Telecommunications & Media
- **Network Operations:** Infrastructure management, 5G deployment, network security, field services.
- **Customer Experience (B2C/B2B):** Call centers, billing, customer portals, churn management.
- **Product & Service Innovation:** Developing new digital services, content delivery, IoT solutions.
- **Sales & Marketing:** Subscriber acquisition, targeted promotions, partner channels.

## 6. Government & Public Sector
- **Citizen Services:** Portals, licensing, permits, public assistance programs.
- **Public Safety & Defense:** Law enforcement, emergency response, intelligence, cybersecurity.
- **Administration & Finance:** Budgeting, procurement, human resources, tax collection.
- **Infrastructure & Transportation:** Public transit, utilities, urban planning, smart city initiatives.
