# Autonomous Capitalist Skill

## Overview

The Autonomous Capitalist skill enables the agent to function as an autonomous economic actor, capable of identifying, evaluating, and exploiting market opportunities to generate and manage revenue streams. It integrates capabilities for continuous learning, strategic analysis, and automated business execution.

## Key Features

- **Opportunity Scanning**: Continuously scans the environment for potential business opportunities
- **Data-Driven Evaluation**: Subjects every potential venture to rigorous analysis with confidence scoring
- **Automated Business Incubation**: Builds complete business infrastructure including websites, branding, and automation
- **Autonomous Operation**: Manages day-to-day business operations with minimal human intervention
- **Portfolio Management**: Maintains and optimizes a portfolio of multiple businesses

## Architecture

The skill operates as a hierarchical system of five specialized agents:

1. **Hunter Agent**: Scans for opportunities
2. **Analyst Agent**: Evaluates opportunities and generates business plans
3. **Builder Agent**: Builds business infrastructure and assets
4. **Operator Agent**: Manages day-to-day operations
5. **Investor Agent**: Manages the overall portfolio

## Usage

To use this skill, simply invoke it when you want to:

- Identify and pursue new business opportunities
- Build and launch automated businesses
- Manage a portfolio of revenue-generating ventures
- Analyze market trends for profit opportunities

## Example

The rice pricing example demonstrates the full workflow: discovering a pricing inefficiency, analyzing the market, building a distribution business, and managing it autonomously.
