# Celebrity Brand Strategist

## Overview

This skill provides a systematic framework for creating successful brand ventures with celebrity partners. It guides you through the entire lifecycle of a celebrity-brand partnership, from identifying high-potential candidates to launching and scaling the brand. The methodology is based on a data-driven approach to authentic brand alignment, modeled after successful ventures like Ryan Reynolds' Aviation Gin and Mint Mobile.

## Workflow

This skill follows a five-phase workflow. Follow these steps sequentially to develop and execute your celebrity partnership strategy.

### Phase 1: Market & Candidate Research

**Objective:** To understand the market landscape and identify a preliminary list of potential celebrity partners.

1.  **Research Market Trends:** Use the `search` tool to research the latest trends in celebrity-brand partnerships and identify high-opportunity brand categories. Refer to `/home/ubuntu/skills/celebrity-brand-strategist/references/market_analysis.md` for a detailed overview of key categories.
2.  **Identify Potential Candidates:** Use the `search` tool to find rising stars, influencers, and established celebrities across different tiers (A-List, B-List, Tier C). Focus on individuals with high engagement and authentic connections with their audience.
3.  **Create a Candidate List:** Compile a list of 20-30 potential candidates. For each candidate, gather basic information such as their primary platforms, follower counts, and notable brand partnerships.

### Phase 2: Evaluation & Scoring

**Objective:** To objectively evaluate and score potential celebrity partners using a data-driven framework.

1.  **Use the Evaluation Framework:** Refer to `/home/ubuntu/skills/celebrity-brand-strategist/references/evaluation_framework.md` for the detailed evaluation criteria and scoring matrix.
2.  **Score Each Candidate:** For each candidate on your list, assign a score from 1-5 for each of the five evaluation pillars: Audience Alignment, Engagement & Influence, Brand Compatibility, Business Acumen, and Growth Potential.
3.  **Calculate Weighted Score:** Use the formula in the evaluation framework to calculate a final weighted score for each candidate.
4.  **Rank Candidates:** Rank the candidates based on their total score to create a prioritized list of top-tier prospects.

### Phase 3: Category Matching

**Objective:** To match your top-ranked celebrity candidates with the most promising brand categories.

1.  **Use the Matching Methodology:** Refer to `/home/ubuntu/skills/celebrity-brand-strategist/references/matching_methodology.md` for the detailed matching criteria and scoring matrix.
2.  **Identify Potential Categories:** For each of your top 3-5 candidates, brainstorm 2-3 potential brand categories that align with their personal brand and audience.
3.  **Score Each Pairing:** Use the matching matrix to score each celebrity-category pairing based on Authenticity, Audience Fit, Competitive Differentiation, and Market Opportunity.
4.  **Select Final Pairings:** Based on the composite scores, select the most promising celebrity-brand pairing(s) to move forward with.

### Phase 4: Pitch & Negotiation

**Objective:** To develop a compelling pitch and negotiate a partnership agreement.

1.  **Develop a Pitch Deck:** Use the pitch deck template at `/home/ubuntu/skills/celebrity-brand-strategist/templates/pitch_deck_template.md` to create a customized pitch for your target celebrity.
2.  **Initiate Outreach:** Identify the celebrity's representation (agent, manager) and initiate contact with a concise, high-impact teaser.
3.  **Conduct Pitch Meetings:** Present your pitch, focusing on authentic alignment, mutual value creation, and the long-term vision for the brand.
4.  **Negotiate Partnership Terms:** Work with legal counsel to negotiate and finalize a partnership agreement, including equity stakes, roles and responsibilities, and creative control.

### Phase 5: Brand Launch & Scaling

**Objective:** To successfully launch the brand and scale operations.

1.  **Develop the Brand:** Collaborate with your celebrity partner to develop the brand identity, product, and marketing strategy.
2.  **Execute the Launch:** Implement a coordinated go-to-market strategy, led by a major PR and social media push from your celebrity partner.
3.  **Monitor & Optimize:** Track key performance indicators (KPIs) and use data to iterate on your product, marketing, and operations.
4.  **Scale the Business:** Explore new product lines, geographic markets, and retail channels to grow the brand.
