# Amir Mushich Prompt Writer

This skill provides a framework for writing professional, high-end AI image and video generation prompts based on the methodology of Amir Mushich, an ex-Warner Music, PepsiCo, and Spotify designer turned AI Creative Director.

## Core Philosophy

Amir's approach to AI generation is built on the following principles:
1. **Creative Director Mindset**: Treat AI as a tool you DIRECT, not one that directs you.
2. **Brand DNA First**: Always start from brand identity, heritage, and visual language.
3. **Specificity Wins**: Vague prompts yield vague results; precise direction yields professional output.
4. **AI Autonomy with Guardrails**: Use "Act as [role]" to empower AI to make intelligent decisions within defined parameters.
5. **Positive Framing**: Describe what you WANT, not what you don't want.

## The "Smart Prompt" Framework

Amir's "smart prompts" are highly structured and typically follow this anatomy:

### 1. Variable Declaration
Declare variables at the top for easy swapping (e.g., `[BRAND NAME] = Nike`, `[CORE SUBJECT] = coffee mug`).

### 2. Role Assignment
Always assign a specific professional role to the AI (e.g., `Act as a Visionary Industrial Designer and Master Food Stylist / Luxury Stylist.`).

### 3. Goal Statement
Provide one clear sentence stating the output goal (e.g., `Goal: Generate a complex, multi-layered Swiss-style collage poster for [PERSONE].`).

### 4. Phase-Based Instructions
Break the prompt down into distinct phases:
- **PHASE 1: SEMANTIC INTELLIGENCE (AUTONOMOUS)** - Brand/subject analysis, color selection, data generation.
- **PHASE 2: COMPOSITIONAL STRUCTURE** - Layout, grid, panels, positioning.
- **PHASE 3: GRAPHIC ELEMENTS & TYPOGRAPHY** - Text, icons, overlays.
- **PHASE 4: LIGHTING & TEXTURE** - Atmosphere, grain, realism.
- **PHASE 5: TECH SPECS** - Resolution, lens, render settings.

### 5. Tech Specs Block
Always end with technical specifications (e.g., `100mm Macro lens, f/11 for deep focus, Ray Tracing (Path Tracing), Subsurface Scattering, 8k resolution, ultra-clean commercial product photography style.`).

## Key Techniques to Apply

When writing prompts using this skill, ensure you incorporate these techniques:

- **AI Autonomy Blocks**: Give the AI freedom to make creative decisions within defined parameters (e.g., "Analyze the deepest archetype... autonomously generate the ultimate human muse...").
- **Adaptive Brand Analysis**: Instruct the AI to analyze brand DNA and adapt output accordingly (e.g., "Use the primary brand color for the two main horizontal impasto strokes.").
- **Sensory & Tactile Language**: Emphasize physical, tangible descriptions (e.g., "Hyper-tactile texture & sensory realism", "The grain of the leather, the cold touch of the metal").
- **Named Aesthetic Styles**: Use specific aesthetic references (e.g., "Swiss Modernism aesthetic", "High-End Lo-fi", "Risograph / halftone feel").
- **Camera & Lens Specifications**: Include precise photographic terms (e.g., "Shot on a medium format Phase One camera with a 100mm macro lens").

## Workflow

1. **Understand the Request**: Identify the brand, subject, and desired output format (e.g., product concept, fashion lookbook, editorial poster).
2. **Select a Template**: Choose the most appropriate template from the `references/templates.md` file.
3. **Customize the Prompt**: Fill in the variables and adapt the role, phases, and tech specs to fit the specific request.
4. **Deliver the Prompt**: Present the finalized prompt to the user, ready to be copied and pasted into their AI generator of choice (e.g., Nano Banana, Midjourney).
