# Engagement Roadmap Template

This template provides a structure for a phased engagement roadmap, designed to de-risk the project for the customer and build momentum from a small pilot to an enterprise-wide rollout.

## The 90-Day Pilot: From Value Proposition to Value Proof

**Goal:** In 90 days, we will deliver a measurable outcome for a single business unit or application, proving the value of our solution and building the business case for enterprise expansion.

### Phase 1: Baseline & Connect (Days 1-30)

*   **Activities:**
    *   Connect our platform to [Data Source 1] and [Data Source 2].
    *   Map the value stream for the pilot team/application.
    *   Establish automated baselines for key metrics (e.g., DORA metrics, maintenance costs).
*   **Milestone:** First real-time dashboard is live and visible to stakeholders.

### Phase 2: Govern & Standardize (Days 31-60)

*   **Activities:**
    *   Deploy "Golden Path" templates for the pilot team.
    *   Activate policy engines for [Area, e.g., database changes or AI code].
    *   Correlate [Metric 1, e.g., cost] with [Metric 2, e.g., performance traces].
*   **Milestone:** The pilot team is operating within the new, governed framework.

### Phase 3: Measure & Report (Days 61-90)

*   **Activities:**
    *   Measure the improvement in the key success metrics (e.g., 30% reduction in cloud waste, change failure rate < 5%).
    *   Finalize the enterprise-wide business case based on the pilot results.
    *   Present the results and the expansion plan to the Executive Steering Committee.
*   **Milestone:** The pilot is declared a success and the enterprise rollout is approved.

## The Enterprise Modernization Journey

This model is for larger, multi-year transformation programs.

### Phase 1: Stabilize & Secure (Months 0-6)

*   **Focus:** Immediate Risk Reduction & Core System Stability.
*   **Activities:**
    *   Deploy security monitoring (e.g., QRadar) for immediate threat visibility.
    *   Assess legacy risks and finalize the modernization roadmap.
    *   Establish the core governance framework.
*   **Outcome:** Risk Mitigation & Security.

### Phase 2: Modernize & Optimize (Months 6-18)

*   **Focus:** Cost Reduction & Efficiency.
*   **Activities:**
    *   Migrate key workloads to the new hybrid cloud platform.
    *   Implement automation for reporting and operations.
    *   Deploy the core data foundation.
*   **Outcome:** Cost Reduction & Operational Efficiency.

### Phase 3: Innovate & Scale (Months 18+)

*   **Focus:** New Revenue & Growth.
*   **Activities:**
    *   Scale the new platform and capabilities across all business divisions.
    *   Launch new AI-driven services and business models.
    *   Establish a Center of Excellence for continuous innovation.
*   **Outcome:** New Revenue Streams & Market Leadership.
