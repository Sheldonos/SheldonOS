# Strategic Plays Library

This library contains a set of pre-defined, integrated solutions that address common, high-value enterprise challenges. Each "play" combines multiple products and services into a cohesive narrative that is easy for a customer to understand and buy.

## 1. The Modernization Play

*   **Customer Challenge:** "Our legacy systems are slow, expensive to maintain, and prevent us from innovating."
*   **Narrative:** Transform monolithic legacy applications (COBOL, WebSphere, SAP) into cloud-native microservices while maintaining business continuity and reducing costs.
*   **Core Components:**
    *   **Assessment:** IBM Consulting Application Modernization / Application Modernization Accelerator (AMA)
    *   **Platform:** Cloud Pak for Applications (CP4Apps) on Red Hat OpenShift
    *   **Runtimes:** WebSphere Hybrid Edition / Liberty, Quarkus
    *   **Optimization:** IBM Turbonomic
*   **Key Metrics:**
    *   40-60% Reduction in Licensing Costs
    *   30-40% Faster Release Cycles
    *   50% Lower Maintenance Overhead

## 2. The AI-Powered Development Play

*   **Customer Challenge:** "We need to increase developer productivity and accelerate feature delivery, but we need to do it in a governed, secure way."
*   **Narrative:** Empower your development teams with enterprise-grade AI to double productivity, automate repetitive tasks, and refactor legacy code, all within a secure and compliant framework.
*   **Core Components:**

*   **Key Metrics:**
    *   50%+ Increase in Developer Productivity
    *   40%+ Faster Feature Delivery
    *   100% Governed and Compliant AI

## 3. The Engineering Velocity Play

*   **Customer Challenge:** "Our engineering processes are fragmented across different teams and platforms (especially cloud vs. mainframe), slowing down our time-to-market for complex products."
*   **Narrative:** Unify the entire engineering lifecycle, from requirements to code to validation, across all platforms. Create a single source of truth and a fully automated CI/CD pipeline for complex, regulated industries.
*   **Core Components:**
    *   **Requirements & Design:** IBM Engineering Lifecycle Management (ELM)
    *   **CI/CD:** IBM DevOps Suite
    *   **Mainframe Integration:** IBM Z for GitLab
*   **Key Metrics:**
    *   70% Reduction in Cycle Time
    *   50% Reduction in Defect Rate
    *   100% Unified View across Cloud and Mainframe

## 4. The Data Resilience & AI-Ready Infrastructure Play

## 5. The Cybersecurity / IT-OT Convergence Play

*   **Customer Challenge:** "Our operational technology (OT) is now connected to our IT network, creating massive new security vulnerabilities that we can't see or control."
*   **Narrative:** Gain unified visibility and threat management across your entire IT and OT landscape. Protect critical infrastructure from cyber threats and ensure operational continuity.
*   **Core Components:**
    *   **Threat Detection:** IBM Security QRadar SIEM
    *   **Automated Response:** IBM Security QRadar SOAR
    *   **Data Security:** IBM Guardium
    *   **Identity:** IBM Security Verify
*   **Key Metrics:**
    *   Prevent $5M+ per-incident breach costs
    *   Unified IT/OT visibility
    *   Automated threat response
